VGMF Scanner for Android

Staff check-in app for scanning participant e-tickets at the Autism Awareness Programme. Requires a scanner account from admin.

Download APK (Android) Use scanner in browser instead
  1. Download the APK file to your phone.
  2. If prompted, allow Install unknown apps for your browser or Files app.
  3. Open the APK and tap Install.
  4. Sign in with your scanner staff email and password (create account in Admin → Staff users).
  5. Allow camera permission when asked — needed for QR scanning.

Staff only. This app loads the live scanner from autism.vaidyagogate.org so UI updates apply without reinstalling.
